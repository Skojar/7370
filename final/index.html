About Dice Rolls

Click on the images of the dice to roll 2d6. Click on a bonus to roll 2d6+[bonus]. Roll Results are interpreted according to the Powered by the Apocalypse standard categories:

  • ✖ = Failure
  • ❓ = Partial Success
  • ★ = Success
  • ♛ = Advanced Success
